Cars

The moive Cars is a movie about a red race car named Lightning. The moive starts off at a race where you'll meet the main character Lightning McQueen, a little bit later Lightning will have to stop in a small town. While he is in this town he is forced to fix the road he ruined, after spending time in this small town he will start to like the people in it. He will meet his new best friend Mater, his girlfriend Sally, and his teacher Doc. Even if Lightning wants to stay in this town he still has to compete in a race, when Ligthning is at this race his family or the people from the town come to see him and his teacher will coach him in the race. When Lightning is in the race he will be faced with a choice where he will have to choose wether he wants to win the race or help one of the other (The King) racers. Lightning will choose to help the other racer, while Lightning lost the race he leared that he doesn't always have to come in first place.
